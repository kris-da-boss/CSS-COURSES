CLUB & SOCIETIES

Beyound subject matter knowledge,our co-curricular activities are designed to promote the development of the social skills, moral values, physical development and also for enhancing the learning process of the students. it is therefore compulsory that every student participates in these activities. These activities are organised through clubs and the societies which include: Music club coding club literally, debating & Dramatic Society JETS club, Bible club, Home Making club Every student has the chioce of being in at least one club based on interest or ability.

STUDENT'S GOVERMENT

The Students' Parliament is a student governing council which consist of reprsentatives of the student nominated from all classes. This council together with the body of prefects is charged with providing leadership among students. Through this initative, students develop the skills of diplomacy, organization and leadership while promoting students' participation in school management

LEADERSHIP & MENTORING

Our special one-hour leadership and mentoring sessions with students which is sometimes organized in small groups for the more activities participation, bring to life topics covering teens, health talks, social challenges, personal development and practical wisdom for the living. Facillitators challenges, personal development and practical wisdom for living. Facillitators are drawn from certifield life coaches, experienced mentors and youth leaders with track of sucecess. through this programme, students develop a moral compass and the strengh of character to lead productive lives.
